My Favorite Hobby

My favorite hobby of all time is golfing. I got into golfing during COVID and became addicted to it. The feeling of hitting a great golf shot is one that you remember. Continuously getting better is also a driving force in why I play so often. It is a game that is very visible when you are improving and seeing that improvement feels amazing.

The Clubs I Play
